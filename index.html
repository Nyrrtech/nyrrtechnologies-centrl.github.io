Questions

News Sentiment Radar FAQ

What is news sentiment analysis?

News sentiment analysis uses AI to classify whether a story, headline, or topic is positive, negative, or neutral, and can also identify emotions such as anger, fear, joy, and surprise.

How does this help SEO and content planning?

It helps teams identify trending topics, keyword opportunities, content gaps, and changing audience narratives before creating search-focused briefs or articles.

Can the app generate article briefs?

Yes. Pro workflows can turn a story or topic into a structured editorial brief and draft, giving writers a faster starting point while keeping human review in the process.

Which teams use this kind of tool?

Newsrooms, publishers, agencies, content marketers, analysts, and research teams can use it to monitor signals, compare sources, and create timely editorial content.
